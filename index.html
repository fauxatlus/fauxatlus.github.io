About Me

My name is Randy Littlefield. I am a 28 year old Savannah local. I currently attend Georgia Tech and am working on a certification to become a Full Stack Web Developer. In my free time, I enjoy photography, cooking, coding, and building web pages. I enjoy learning new skills, I’m not afraid to tackle any task, and I’m willing to relocate for the right job.

Photography work includes headshots, graduation photos, engagement photos, and family photos. I feel that everyone should be able to acquire a high quality remembrance of special moments in their lives, so I offer pay on a sliding scale so that it is affordable for everyone.

I am also happy to work with any non-profit organizations that are in need of front-end web development tasks. .

I have always thought of myself to be very creative, and I’m not afraid to break into any creative project. I love tackling new projects in order to learn new things. Nothing is too big or too small..
